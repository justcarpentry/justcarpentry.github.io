About Me

Hi, I'm Giles Grierson, manager of Just Carpentry. I have been in the building trade since my teens, I'm based in Norwich and I mostly work in and around Norfolk.

I developed my skills over years as an apprentice and am ‘City & Guilds’ qualified. Recent reviews of my work can be found on my mybuilder.com profile. I specialise in creating space saving solutions for your home but also cover:

  • Custom made cabinets
  • Bespoke joinery
  • Doors
  • Floors
  • Cut roofs
  • Timber cladding
  • Kitchens
  • 1st and 2nd fix carpentry

My work brings together a talent for design, craftsmanship and a love of problem solving to help create the home you want to live in.
